Privacy Policy

App: mandarinshifu · Last updated: 25 June 2026

Mandarin Shifu (中文師父) is a Chinese-language education brand. The mandarinshifu application ("the App") is a private tool used by the brand operator to publish our own educational short videos to our own TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API. This policy explains what the App accesses and how that information is handled.

Information the App accesses

With your explicit authorization through TikTok Login, the App requests the video.upload permission. This allows the App to send a video file to the authorizing account's own TikTok inbox (drafts). The App also receives the OAuth access and refresh tokens that TikTok issues to perform this action. The App does not access your followers, direct messages, watch history, or any personal data belonging to other people.

How the information is used

Tokens are used solely to upload the operator's own short videos to the operator's own TikTok account. The App does not use the information for any other purpose, and does not perform analytics, profiling, or advertising.

Storage and security

OAuth tokens are stored only on the operator's own computer, in a permission-restricted local file. They are never uploaded to, or stored on, any external server controlled by us.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose any accessed data to third parties.

Retention and deletion

Tokens are retained until they expire or are revoked. You may delete them at any time by removing the local token file, and you can revoke the App's access in TikTok under Settings & privacy → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions.

Children

The App is an internal publishing tool and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
